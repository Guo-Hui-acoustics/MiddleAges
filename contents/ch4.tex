\lettrine{T}{ragedy} struck when the Warmaster Horus, 
the Emperor's most beloved son, 
fell to the corruption of Chaos. 
He convinced half the legions to turn their bolters against their brothers.

\section{The Siege of Terra}
The galaxy burned for seven years. 
The traitor forces eventually reached Terra itself. 
In the final duel aboard the \textit{Vengeful Spirit}, 
the Emperor slew Horus but was mortally wounded. 
He ascended to the Golden Throne, where he has remained for ten thousand years.
\sidenote{The Price: To keep the Astronomican lit, a thousand psykers are sacrificed daily to the Throne.}
